#  HR Employee Attrition Dashboard

> An interactive HR analytics dashboard built in Tableau Public analysing employee attrition patterns across 1,470 IBM employees.

 **[Live Dashboard](https://public.tableau.com/app/profile/farwa.irfan/viz/HREmployeeAttritionDashboard_17738490103850/HREmployeeAttritionAnalysis)**

---

##  Dashboard Overview

4-chart interactive dashboard revealing why employees leave — built with dark theme for maximum visual impact.

| Chart | Insight |
|---|---|
| Attrition by Department | R&D loses most employees (133), Sales has highest rate |
| Attrition by Age Group | 20-30 year olds churn most |
| Attrition by Job Role | Sales Representatives have highest attrition rate |
| Income vs Attrition | Employees who leave earn ~$2,000/month less |

---

##  Key Business Insights

- **16.1% overall attrition rate** — 237 out of 1,470 employees left
- **Research & Development** loses the most people in absolute numbers (133)
- **Sales Representatives** have the highest attrition rate by job role
- **Employees who leave earn ~$4,800/month** vs $6,800 for those who stay — a $2,000 gap
- **Age 20-30** is the highest risk group — younger employees churn significantly more

---

##  Dashboard Features

- Dark navy theme for professional presentation
- Color-coded attrition — Navy (retained) vs Red (churned)
- Interactive filters — click any chart to filter the entire dashboard
- 4 coordinated views telling a complete HR story
